Prodigal (Adjective)

spending money freely and rather wastefully

फ़िज़ूलख़र्च, अपव्‍ययी, ख़र्चीला

Synonyms

extravagant, high-rolling, profligate, spendthrift, squandering, thriftless, unthrifty, wasteful

Antonyms

cheap, close, closefisted, mean, miserly, niggardly, parsimonious, penurious, pinching, spare, sparing, stingy, stinting, tight, tightfisted

Examples

  1. We sipped our beers and wondered at one another, at what was left of all that and of those prodigal days.

  2. The prodigal use of antibiotics in animals has the same consequence as their overprescription for human beings.
